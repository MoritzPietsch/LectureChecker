The Credit System

The LectureChecker aims to help you decide which lectures to attend at TUM.

However, the LectureChecker only works if everyone contributes by evaluating lectures!

Therefore you can only access the download-links to exercises or summaries provided by other users with enough credit points.

Getting Credit Points
Action Reward
Give a quantitative rating of a lecture
Provide the average of the test you wrote
Write a short verbal comment
Upload a download-link to a summary, exercises or other lecture-relevant material you would like to share with others

Value of Attendance

Describes if you recommend attending the lecture or course personally compared to preparing for the exam with provided materials only.

Result/Work-Ratio

Describes how much work you have to invest to achieve a good result in the final exam. Little work for a good grade means the ratio is high!

Exam Easiness

Describes if the exam was hard or easy to do.
